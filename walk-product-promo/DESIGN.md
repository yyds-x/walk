# Walk Promo Design

## Brand Summary
Walk is a WeChat mini-program that turns daily walking, sign-ins, task completion, and friend invites into vitality points and redeemable welfare. The promo should feel energetic, friendly, and reward-driven.

## Colors
- `#f5fff6` Fresh page background
- `#dff9f0` Mint panels and calm activity areas
- `#1fb66b` Primary action green
- `#143322` Deep green text and phone frame
- `#e8433f` Reward red and urgency accents
- `#ffd65a` Coin and vitality highlight
- `#fff0c9` Warm reward surface
- `#47705b` Secondary text

## Typography
Use system Chinese UI fonts: `"PingFang SC", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif`. Keep headlines heavy, rounded, and direct. Letter spacing stays at `0`.

## Components
Use mini-program-inspired phone frames, vitality coin imagery, rounded task rows, compact product cards, sign-in tiles, and step progress tracks. Cards can be rounded because they represent actual app UI; page sections should be full-frame bands.

## Motion
Motion should feel springy and useful: phone panels slide in, task rows stack in, coins pulse, runner limbs loop, and roads drift to imply walking. Use GSAP timelines only and finite repeats.

## What Not To Do
Do not use generic SaaS blues or purple gradients. Do not show abstract app mockups without Walk-specific features. Do not use dark full-screen gradients. Do not make a landing page; the video starts inside the product story.

## Site Summary
Name: Walk. Top colors: mint green, action green, reward red, vitality yellow, deep green text. Fonts: Chinese system UI. Key assets: vitality coin, welfare bubbles, red packet, task icons, gift icon. Vibe: a bright, everyday rewards loop for walking and simple habits.
